What is "What's for Dinner?"

"What's for Dinner?" is a educational project developed by a team of Rutgers Bootcamp students, Kobe, Kyle and Erica. Its inspiration came from being stuck at home during the Covid-19(Corona Virus) Outbreak. This website is powered by 2 APIs, TheMealDB and TheCocktailDB, and is styled using Bootstrap, Animate.css and Anime.js.
